Schemes

Donation

The Donation Scheme is designed to assist patients who require financial support for their medical treatment. This scheme allows individuals or organizations to contribute funds to help cover the medical expenses of patients who are unable to afford treatment on their own.

Loan

The Loan Scheme offers a financial solution for patients who need immediate medical treatment but lack the necessary funds. Through this scheme, patients can obtain loans with flexible repayment options to cover their medical expenses. It provides access to timely healthcare services without the need for upfront payment.

Meal

The Meal Scheme ensures that patients and their loved ones are well taken care of during their stay. With wholesome and balanced meals served with care and consideration, we aim to alleviate stress and provide comfort so that the families could focus on what truly matters- their health and recovery.

IVF

The IVF Scheme is tailored for couples struggling with infertility issues who desire to start a family through assisted reproductive technology. This scheme offers specialized fertility treatments, such as in vitro fertilization (IVF), at subsidized rates or with financial assistance. It provides hope and support to couples facing fertility challenges, helping them fulfill their dream of parenthood.

Adoption

The Adoption Scheme facilitates the adoption process for individuals or couples wishing to expand their family through adoption. This scheme offers guidance, support, and financial assistance. It streamlines the adoption process, covering legal fees, counseling services, and other associated costs, thereby making adoption a viable option for those seeking to provide a loving home to children in need of a family.
